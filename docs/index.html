About

My name is Alex(andria),
a Utrecht-based game and interactive narrative designer with her roots in D&D homebrew

As a recent graduate with a BSc in Creative Media and Game Technologies, I'm currently applying for a master's.

My interest lies in providing insight through interaction, whether it be interactive stories or experiences.

For 20 weeks I have worked as an intern game/narrative designer at FourceLabs, contributing to the game Unexplored 2.

I hope my work catches your fancy, and feel free to shoot me a message about whatever!

The Anders Cyclopaedia

(Graduation Project)

Lexicology and Exploration

About

An experiment of merging digital and pen & paper gameplay. Explore a parallel world and record its denizens to your Cyclopaedia.

  • Genre: Third-Person, Exploration, Puzzle
  • Engine: Unity
  • Duration: 20 weeks
  • Itch.io link: The Anders Cyclopaedia
  • Companion Paper: No Stupid Questions

Contributions

  • Visual Design

    • Modelling and texturing a 3D player model.
    • Modelling and texturing 4 Andersian creatures.
    • Modelling and texturing various environmental assets.

  • Level Design

    • Designing 3 explorable areas.

  • Animation

    • Rigging and animating each creature.
    • Rigging and making a few custom animations for the player character.

  • Game Design

    • Establishing the core premise of the game.
    • Designing 5 main areas, each with their own puzzles and level elements.
    • Designing the Cyclopaedia aspect of the game.

  • Narrative Design

    • Writing all of the game's dialogue.
    • Implementing narrative "pipe" scenes between explorable areas.
    • Writing the Cyclopaedia, with the addition of narrative tidbits to be used in puzzles.

  • Audio Design

    • Production and implementation of various sound effects.
    • Production of 1 musical track.

The Puppeteer's Troupe

(Cancelled)

Graduation Project

About

For my graduation project I planned to develop a tabletop RPG which aimed to trigger critical thought about gender roles and gender expression in its participants; and using tech as a tool to break through the medium's pen & paper traditions to achieve this goal as effectively as possible.

  • Genre: Tabletop RPG
  • Engine: 20 weeks
  • Duration: 20 weeks
  • Artefact Document

Contributions

  • Game Design

    • Designing a character creation system which imposes roles onto the players.
    • Designing 3 archetypes (or classes) for playtesting purposes.
    • Designing game rules.

  • Narrative Design

    • Establishing a narrative theme in collaboration with a visual artist.
    • Developing an NPC playbook for the main antagonist of the experience, The Puppeteer.

Maintenance

(Shelved)

"Take your self, you'll be okay."

About

Maintenance is a psychological survival horror game. Though in the vein of classics such as Silent Hill, Kuon, Rule of Rose, and Eternal Darkness, it strives to carve its own path using contemporary themes as inspiration for its narrative.

  • Genre: Survival Horror
  • Engine: Unity
  • Duration: Ongoing (Paused)
  • Design Bible

Contributions

  • Game Development

    • Creating a flexible fixed camera system.
    • Creating an inventory system.

  • Game Design

    • Maintaining a comprehensive design bible.
    • Designing multiple puzzles.
    • Designing multiple enemies.

  • Narrative Design

    • Writing NPC dialogue.
    • Writing item descriptions
    • Designing and implementing narrative-driven systems and mechanics.

  • Visual Design

    • Character design, modelling, texturing, and animation.
    • Environmental and prop design.
    • UI design
