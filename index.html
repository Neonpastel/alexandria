My name is Alex(andria),

a Utrecht-based game and interactive narrative designer with her roots in D&D homebrew

 

As a recent graduate with a BSc in Creative Media and Game Technologies, I'm currently applying for a master's.
 

My interest lies in providing insight through interaction, whether it be interactive stories or experiences.
